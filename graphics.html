Magma Coffee

Packaging designs for a theoretical coffee brand. The idea was to combine different coffee beans and roasts with different chillies of varying spice levels to create different flavours of "chillie coffee". I experimented by grinding up dried chillies, mixing them with ground coffee, and brewing both together. The "Slow Burn" flavour was my favorite.



Cloud Chamber Coffee

Another set of designs based on a theoretical coffee brand. This time the branding is based around greek gods, with each god having a different flavour. Design wise, I was mainly experimenting with colour, and trying to create a group of cohesive designs.
