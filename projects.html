Selected Projects

The Urban Design Process: Creating a Creative Space

A visual exploration of the process of designing an urban space in Manchester, focused on walkability, accessibility, and the creative character of the area.

Local Government Internship: Developing Design Codes

An analysis project completed with the Manchester Urban Design Lab to assist with the development of Stockport Council's design codes. I worked primarily on chapter 6c and filled in missing text and images across the document.

Neighbourhood Regeneration: A Storymap

A group project using GIS to analze the conditions of a neighbourhood in East Manchester. A 15 year regeneration strategy is presented using Esri Storymaps.

Growing Food in a Growing City: Planning for Urban Agriculture

My dissertation thesis, exploring the current extent of urban agriculture in Manchester, and its potential to improve food security. This was done through GIS mapping and a structured literature review.
