Hi, I'm

Callum Wilson

I'm an urban designer originally from the UK. I have strong interests in both placemaking and sustainability. I love cities and all the opportunities, events and excitement they can hold. I also love nature, in all it's beauty and diversity. It often seems like the two are antithetical to each other, with urban development necessitating the destruction of natural habitats, but I believe it doesn't have to be that way. It's my goal to work towards the synthesis of these two incredible things, to create a better world for every living thing that calls it home.
